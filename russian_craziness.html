I currently live in Russia and have lived here for the most of my life. It has beautiful nature and I met an increadible amount of friendly, inspiring and dedicated people here. However, the country also has many serious problems. They are very complex and are often hard to communicate without simplifying and generalizing inappropriately. Naturally, this leads to a lot of misrpresentation in the media and puts off any solutions farther away.

Aiming to raise awareness and understanding of the problems, here I give a non-exhaustive list of some very specific and unambiguous facts and stories about this country which I find disturbing, leaving it to the reader to conclude any general patterns. Please inform me if you found any mistakes or fake news, or if you want to add anything to the list.
