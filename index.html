Vova Sosnilo

I am a research scientist in RIKEN iTHEMS in Wako, Japan. Previously I worked at the University of Regensburg in Germany, and Laboratory of Modern Algebra and Applications in Saint Petersburg, Russia

I do research in the fields of homotopy theory and category theory. My main motivation lies in understanding other areas of mathematics through these subjects. Most of my work so far has been applied to studying cohomology theories of algebraic varieties. Here is my CV

I also play noisy and emotional music, currently my main project is pomehi. Also check out my other project АБРАЗИВНЫЕ МАТЕРИАЛЫ that I am working on jointly with Sasha Gudulin
